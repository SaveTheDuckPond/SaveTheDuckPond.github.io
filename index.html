The Ridgewood Wild Duck Pond

The Ridgewood Wild Duck Pond was a beautiful place where many people would come to relax, walk their dogs, play, or just enjoy the lovely scenery. However now the pond isn't nearly as beautiful as it used to be. This is because when Hurricane Sandy hit in 2011 it caused a lot of problems for the pond.

The Problem

When Hurricane Sandy hit, it damaged the weir that directs water from the Saddle River into the pond. There is also a crack in the clay base at the bottom of the pond. Another problem is that the town was taking to much water from wells. However this problem has been fixed, by the town installing hight-pressuer water lines under the roads.
